  • arrow keys or a/s/w/d: fly saucer
  • hold space: activate tractor beam
  • hold shift: activate hyperdrive
  • f: activate shield
  • r: restart after game over

  • Use your tractor beam to collect as many objects as possible while avoiding missiles and the ground. Crates will give you a shield which protects you from one missile strike. Use hyperdrive if you need a boost. Catch dropped items in midair for bonus points!
